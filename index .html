Our Story

A Welcoming Stay
Near Sacred Ground

Indu Paying Guest House sits at 8/34-240E, Sardar Munesvar Road, Buddha Nagar Colony — just minutes from the revered Dhamek Stupa and the tranquil deer park of Sarnath. We offer clean, comfortable rooms with a homely atmosphere, attentive care, and easy access to one of India's most important spiritual sites.

Whether you're here for pilgrimage, study, or sightseeing in Varanasi, our guesthouse is your quiet base amidst the ancient city's energy.
